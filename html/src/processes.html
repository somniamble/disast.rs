3 Level Habits

L3: the absolute best form, most ideal form, best application of the habit

for example, a full 3-4 hour long production session

L2: A lesser form, but still a commitment of some time and energy

e.g. turning on your octatrack, and arranging some patterns, or recording something

L1: The least form of the habit, something that you can always do

e.g. turning on the octatrack and just playing a pattern, or putting a couple of trigs down, and just noticing something

This will keep your momentum going
